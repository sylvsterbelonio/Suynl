CELL LESSON 1: THE FOUNDATION

Because there is no other foundation that anyone can lay on except the one that has already been laid, which is Jesus Christ. - 1 Corinthians 3:11

We believers are like a house whose foundation is stone. And Christ is the foundation stone. The ones apostles and prophets of God are the pillars. You are the other parts of the house. - Ephesians 2:21

However, the foundation that God has laid is still strong, because it is written, "The Lord knows his people." And "Everyone who says he belongs to the Lord is right to forsake all evil." - 2 Timothy 2:19 APSD

And you Lord in the beginning laid the foundations of the earth and the heavens are the works of your hands! - Hebrews 1:10
